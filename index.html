Unit Converter

Conversion Info

mg/L: Milligrams per liter. Often used in water quality.

ppm: Parts per million. Equivalent to mg/L in water.

ppb: Parts per billion. 1 ppm = 1000 ppb.

mol/L: Moles per liter. Used for molar concentrations.

μmol/L: Micromoles per liter. 1 mol/L = 1,000,000 μmol/L.

μeq/L: Microequivalents per liter. Depends on ion charge.
